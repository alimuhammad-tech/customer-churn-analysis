Business Recommendations
Objective

Based on the customer churn analysis, the following recommendations are proposed to reduce customer churn, improve customer satisfaction, and increase long-term revenue.

1. Encourage Long-Term Contracts

Customers with Month-to-Month contracts have the highest churn rate. The company should encourage these customers to switch to One-Year or Two-Year contracts by offering:

Discounted annual plans
Loyalty rewards
Exclusive long-term benefits

Expected Impact: Lower churn and increased customer retention.

2. Improve Competitor Retention Strategies

A significant number of customers leave because competitors provide better devices, pricing, or promotional offers.

The company should:

Regularly monitor competitor pricing
Introduce competitive promotional offers
Upgrade device bundles
Provide exclusive discounts to existing customers

Expected Impact: Reduced customer loss to competitors.

3. Improve Customer Satisfaction

Low customer satisfaction is strongly associated with customer churn.

The company should:

Improve customer support response times
Resolve complaints more efficiently
Collect customer feedback regularly
Monitor satisfaction scores

Expected Impact: Increased customer loyalty and reduced churn.

4. Improve Internet Service Experience

Fiber Optic customers show relatively higher churn compared to other internet types.

Recommended actions:

Improve network reliability
Review pricing strategies
Increase service quality
Provide proactive technical support

Expected Impact: Better customer experience and improved retention.

5. Reward High-Value Customers

Customers with higher Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) contribute significantly to company revenue.

The company should introduce:

Loyalty programs
Premium customer support
Personalized offers
Early renewal discounts

Expected Impact: Increased customer lifetime value and stronger customer relationships.

6. Develop a Churn Prediction Model

The organization should implement a machine learning model to identify customers who are likely to churn before they leave.

Potential models include:

Logistic Regression
Decision Tree
Random Forest
XGBoost

This enables proactive retention campaigns.

7. Personalized Marketing Campaigns

Use customer demographics, contract type, internet type, and satisfaction scores to create targeted marketing campaigns instead of sending identical offers to all customers.

Expected Impact: Higher conversion rates and improved customer engagement.

Conclusion

Implementing these recommendations can significantly reduce customer churn, improve customer satisfaction, and increase long-term business revenue. By combining business intelligence with predictive analytics, organizations can make data-driven decisions that strengthen customer retention and profitability.
